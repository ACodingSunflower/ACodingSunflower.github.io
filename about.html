An unexpected journey

The most beautiful road to travel on is one surrounded by nature with a panoramic view, much like the well known panoramic drive in Mpumalanga. It is a winding road with many unexpected turns. Every hilltop brings forth breath-taking views. This is how my journey to web-dev has been. It was not a linear road, yet it was, and still is, a beautiful road to travel. It took me a while but I have finally found my passion in the tech industry. Problem solving is that treacherous uphill drive that brings forth breath-taking views at the top and what a joy and an adventure it is. My artistic background gives me an edge when it comes to delivering simple yet elegant code. My creativity is an asset when it comes to creatively solving a problem and my attention to detail ensures very little room for bugs. With that being said, if you need a break from coding listen to my music.
